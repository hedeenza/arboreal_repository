Progress Update pwn.college

2024-09-26

Faster Than Expected

  I’ve been going a little faster than expected - probably because I’ve been more time than originally planned - through pwn.college. Linux Luminarium got finished on 2024-09-19, 7 days sooner than the 10 challenges/day rate I had on my original table, and it seems like Optional Refreshers will also be finished ahead of even the fastest timeline. It’s been too much fun and I’ve been learning too much to want to put it down. There are 4 sections:

Optional Refreshers
Section Challenges
Program Misuse 51
Program Interaction 139
Assembly Crash Course 30
Debugging Refresher 8

  As of today, I’ve finished all 51 challenges in Program Misuse and the first 20 in Program interaction. The current rate of completion isn’t sustainable, so this unit will probably take longer than the next week and a half or so it would take at my current rate, but even slowing down a little enough ground has been gained the overall timeline will still be pretty quick. Overall there have been a lot of frustrations trying to figure out exactly how to solve a few of the challenges, but the struggle’s amplification of the learning process has been invaluable. 10/10 so far.
