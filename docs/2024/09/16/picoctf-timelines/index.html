picoCTF Timelines

2024-09-16

Decisions

  After creating the timelines for pwn.college I became interested in what different completion rates would look like for picoCTF as well. For now I’ll continue to post 1 challenge per day, but it’s not often that I’ll only complete one per day. As with pwn.college, it’s very likely that > 5 completed per day will not be feasible, but it’s interesting to see what very accelerated timelines look like.
